Privacy Policy for TabLift

1. Information Collected

TabLift is a lightweight macOS utility that focuses on privacy and minimal data collection. TabLift may collect limited information to help improve the app and ensure functionality. Specifically, TabLift may collect:

  • The app version and build number
  • The version of macOS you are running
  • Basic, anonymous usage statistics (e.g., how often certain features are used)
  • Crash reports (if enabled by the user)

2. Storage

The collected information is stored securely, either locally on your device or, if analytics or crash reporting is enabled, may be transmitted securely to our analytics or crash reporting provider. TabLift does not store or access any personal documents, files, or window contents.

3. How the information is used

The information collected is used solely to improve the app, monitor its health, and guide future development. Usage statistics and crash reports help identify bugs, improve performance, and prioritize new features.

4. Sharing of Information

The information collected by TabLift is never shared with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Data is used exclusively to support the functionality and improvement of the app. If third-party analytics or crash reporting is used, only anonymized and non-identifiable information is transmitted, in accordance with industry best practices.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or data collected by TabLift, please contact me at ghetumihaieduard@gmail.com.

By using the TabLift app, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.
